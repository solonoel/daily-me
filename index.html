Welcome to Daily Me!
Let's get your personalized briefing set up. Here's what you need to do:
1
📁 Add Categories
Categories organize your headlines and vocabulary. Create groups like Business, Sports, or Technology to keep things tidy.
2
🌐 Select Headline Sources (required)
Choose the news sources you want to follow — RSS feeds and YouTube channels. You must enable at least one source before headlines can be fetched.
3
🔍 Add Keywords or 💡 Topics (at least one required)
Keywords and topics tell Daily Me what to look for in headlines.
🔍 Keywords — Exact Match: Used to search YouTube and filter news. A keyword of "Tesla" will only match articles that contain the word "Tesla" exactly.

💡 Topics — Fuzzy Match: Used for news categorization only (not YouTube). A topic like "electric vehicles" will match articles about EVs, electric cars, and related terms — even if those exact words don't appear.
